3. Simulation/ Digital Twin

A digital twin is a virtual simulation of a real-world product, machine, system, or process based on IoT sensor data. It allows businesses to better analyze, understand, and improve the maintenance and performance of products and industrial systems. For instance, an asset operator can use a digital twin to identify a specific malfunctioning part, predict potential issues, and improve uptime.
