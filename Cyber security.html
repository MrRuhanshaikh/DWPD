5.Cybersecurity

Numerous organizations still rely on production systems and management, and that is disconnected or closed. With the use of standard communications protocols and increased connectivity with Industry 4.0, the need to protect manufacturing lines and critical industrial systems from cybersecurity threats increases drastically. As a result, reliable and secure communications and access management of machines and users are a must. During the past year, various industrial equipment vendors have joined forces with cybersecurity companies through partnerships or acquisitions.
